The capital stock of CADU is comprised of 303,266,668 common and nominative shares, without par value, corresponding to the fixed portion of the capital stock. All CADU's shares are registered in a single series at the Registro Nacional de Valores (National Securities Registry).

CADU's shares entitles their holders to the same patrimonial and voting rights.

Each share grants its holder full voting rights and, in particular, the right to cast a vote in any of the Company's General Shareholders' Meetings, whether Ordinary or Extraordinary.
